Community Garden Volunteer

Join our Community Garden Volunteer Initiative to help cultivate green spaces in our neighborhoods! Volunteers will engage in gardening activities, including planting, weeding, watering, and harvesting. The garden produces fresh vegetables and herbs, contributing to local food banks and providing healthy options for community members.

Youth Mentorship Program

Our Youth Mentorship Program pairs volunteers with local youth to provide guidance, support, and positive role modeling. Mentors help youngsters with academic support, life skills, and engaging in enriching activities, fostering their personal and educational growth.

Senior Companion Project

The Senior Companion Project aims to provide companionship and support to elderly members in our community. Volunteers visit seniors, engage in friendly conversations, assist with errands.
